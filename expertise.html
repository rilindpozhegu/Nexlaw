AREAS OF EXPERTISE

NexLaw is a Consultancy engaged in the provision of legal advice and assistance to Shipowners, Charterers, Commodity Traders, Steel Producers, Grain Houses, Underwriters, Shipyards and the Maritime community at large.

NexLaw Areas
of Expertise

Casualty investigation and management

We are specialists in this field having undertaken instructions in excess of 90 ship related casualties since 1998.

New building Contracts

Though the new building market has tailed off significantly since 2008,we have to date assisted various Shipyards and Buyers respectively in giving delivery to and taking delivery of more than 90 new buildings.

Commodity Contracts

We are regularly instructed by commodity traders in relation to various disputes arising under their contracts and our speciality areas are scrap metal, steel products, coal and grain products.

Cargo Claims Recovery

We are retained by Turkey's largest cargo underwriter to advise and assist them in relation to recovery actions..

Submarine Telecom and
Sub-Sea Engineering
projects

Sub-sea cable laying and maintenance projects is one of our speciality areas as is liability disputes relating to damage to submarine fibre optic cables. We advise Contractors in relation to the sub-sea engineering contracts and provision of marine related resources.

Piracy

On behalf of our Shipowner Clients, we have engaged in direct negotiations with the pirates in the Puntland region of Somalia.

Servicing The Needs Of Shipowners And Ship Operators

We provide shipowners and operators with a professional support structure to not only avoid potential disputes but in the event of a dispute or claim, to secure swift resolution thereof. At times when a Vessel encounters problems arising from plying certain routes or difficult regions, our extensive network of correspondents can ably assist in promptly resolving problems as and when they occur.
