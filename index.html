Who We Are

NexLaw is a Consultancy engaged in the provision of legal and commercial advice to Shipowners, Charterers, Commodity Traders, Underwriters, Shipyards and Steel Mills on a global basis.

NexLaw is managed by Ravi Ravindran, who originates from Singapore. Ravi graduated from the University of Wales Institute of Science and Technology, in 1983 with a BSc. Hons. in Maritime Commerce. He obtained his Diploma in Law from City University, London in 1986. Following three years of articleship in London, Ravi successfully completed the Law Society Final Examinations at the College of York in August 1990. He was admitted as a Solicitor in December 1990. After working with the UK P&I Club in London for six years, he worked as the Senior Legal Adviser to Cable & Wireless Marine Ltd and subsequently set up his own Legal Practice in Istanbul, Turkey.

NexLaw is also complemented by Turkish qualified lawyers who undertake Turkish law related matters within the Turkish jurisdiction. We also work with Emirati Advocates and Legal Consultants in respect of those matters involving the UAE jurisdiction.
